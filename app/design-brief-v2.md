# Digital Cosmos v2.0 — DREV SPACE Quantum Universe Portal

## Creative Mandate
This is not a website. This is a **living digital universe** where the visitor enters, explores, and travels through space. Every pixel serves immersion. Every interaction feels physical. The browser becomes a futuristic WebGL universe.

## Visual DNA
Apple Vision Pro spatial design × NASA mission control × Unreal Engine cinematic realism × clean cyberpunk × Pixar-level wonder.

Avoid: dark dystopian sci-fi, military aesthetics, cluttered interfaces, generic space backgrounds.

## Opening Sequence
1. Black screen. Quantum particles slowly activate.
2. Glowing cyan energy field forms in darkness.
3. Camera accelerates through a tunnel of stars at impossible speed — past nebula clouds, energy waves, holographic particles.
4. Massive solar system emerges.
5. "EXPLORE THE DIGITAL COSMOS" materializes from glowing particles — typography floats like a holographic command interface.

## The 3D Universe
- 5,000+ animated stars, volumetric cosmic dust, glowing nebula clouds
- Realistic lighting, particle explosions, gravitational energy effects
- Cinematic bloom, depth of field, atmospheric fog
- Sun: animated solar flares, plasma movement, glowing corona, realistic light scattering
- 8 planets that feel like destinations, not objects

## Planet Experiences
- **Earth:** Cinematic orbital fly-by. Blue atmosphere, moving clouds, city light networks, holographic global grid. "DREV GLOBAL STEM NETWORK" interface with glowing connection points.
- **Mars:** Futuristic exploration scene — colonies, robotics labs, autonomous drones, AI research stations, rover technology.
- **Saturn:** Camera flies through rings. Thousands of particles surround the viewer. Rings become a navigation highway.

## Navigation
- Drag: rotate around planets, tilt camera, explore angles
- Scroll: zoom from galaxy → planetary orbit → surface level
- Click: launch cinematic camera flight
- Every transition feels like Star Wars holographic navigation × NASA mission control × Apple Vision Pro spatial computing

## DREV Command Interface
Floating holographic dashboard: transparent glass panels, cyan glowing borders, animated data streams, AI navigation assistant, orbital coordinates, planet info. Feels like a spaceship cockpit.

## Content Sections (universe transforms, doesn't change pages)
1. **What Is Digital Cosmos:** Floating holographic classroom in space. Students connect through DREV technology. Robots, drones, AI systems, space exploration.
2. **DREV Mission Worlds:** Interactive planet cards floating in 3D space. Clicking launches camera into that world.
3. **Final Launch Portal:** Camera leaves solar system. Galaxy rotates behind user. Giant DREV portal appears.

## Technical Stack
React 19 + Three.js + React Three Fiber + WebGL/GLSL shaders + GSAP cinematic transitions + Lenis smooth scroll. 60 FPS target.
